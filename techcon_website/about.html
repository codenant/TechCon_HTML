History

TechCon is an annual technology conference that brings together the brightest minds in the industry to share insights, inspire innovation, and foster collaboration. Since its inception in 2010, TechCon has grown to become a must-attend event for professionals across the tech spectrum.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for tech enthusiasts to connect, learn, and grow. We aim to showcase the latest developments in technology and provide attendees with the tools and knowledge they need to drive the industry forward.

Past Speakers

Sundar Pichai - CEO of Google and Alphabet - Speaker at TechCon 2015
Pichai joined Google in 2004, where he led the product management and innovation efforts for a suite of Google's client software products, including Google Chrome and ChromeOS, as well as being largely responsible for Google Drive.

Sheryl Sandberg - COO of Facebook - Speaker at TechCon 2021
In June 2012, she was elected to Facebook's board of directors, becoming the first woman to serve on its board. As head of the company's advertising business, Sandberg was credited for making the company profitable.
